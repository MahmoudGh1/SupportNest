THE PROBLEM
Customer support is the #1 cost center for SaaS and e-commerce businesses. Average resolution time is 24–48 hours for complex issues. Support agents spend 70% of time on Tier 1 issues that could be automated. Customer churn spikes 3x after a bad support experience. Hiring costs $40K+/agent/year.
THE SOLUTION
SupportNest deploys a hierarchy of specialized AI agents that handle the full support lifecycle — from instant Tier 1 resolution to intelligent Tier 2 troubleshooting to human escalation with full context — reducing support costs by 70% while improving CSAT.
KEY BENEFIT: Resolve 80% of support tickets instantly, 24/7, with higher satisfaction scores than human-only teams.
